# SOAP Note

Generate a SOAP note for the patient's most recent encounter. Use this structure:

## Subjective
- Chief complaint / reason for visit
- History of present illness
- Review of symptoms

## Objective
- Vital signs (if available)
- Recent lab results
- Physical exam findings (based on encounter data)

## Assessment
- Active diagnoses with clinical reasoning
- Problem list prioritized by acuity

## Plan
- Medication changes or continuations
- Lab orders / follow-up tests needed
- Referrals if indicated
- Follow-up timeline
