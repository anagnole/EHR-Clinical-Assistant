# Referral Letter

Write a clinical referral letter for the patient. Include:

- Patient demographics (name, age, gender)
- Relevant medical history (active conditions)
- Current medications
- Recent relevant lab results
- Reason for referral
- Specific questions for the specialist

Format as a formal clinical letter with:
- Date
- From: [Referring physician]
- To: [Specialist]
- RE: [Patient name]
- Body with clinical summary
- Signature line
